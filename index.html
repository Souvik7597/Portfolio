About Me

Hi, I'm Souvik Ghosh. A passionate UI/UX Designer and Frontend Developer. I love crafting clean, user-focused digital experiences. As a fresher, I bring fresh eyes and eager energy. Design is how I think development is how I build. I focus on simplicity, usability, and clarity. From wireframes to websites, I enjoy every step. I design in Figma and bring it to life with code...... HTML, CSS, JavaScript and my tools to create. I'm learning React and always exploring more. Responsive design isn't optional it's essential. I care about color, spacing, and typography. I believe good UX solves real problems. Feedback fuels my growth. Every project is a chance to improve. I may be new, but I'm here to make an impact. I value curiosity, consistency, and collaboration. I build with intention, design with empathy. Let's create experiences people love to use. Welcome to my portfolio—my journey starts here.
